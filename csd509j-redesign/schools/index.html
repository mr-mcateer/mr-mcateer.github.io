Programs Across Our Schools

Specialized programs that enrich the learning experience for Corvallis students

Gifted Education

TAG Program

The Talented and Gifted program identifies and serves students who perform or show potential for performing at remarkably high levels of accomplishment across all district schools.

Language

Dual Language Immersion

Students at Adams, Garfield, and Lincoln Elementary develop bilingual and biliterate skills through instruction in both English and Spanish beginning in kindergarten.

Career Readiness

CTE Pathways

Corvallis High and Crescent Valley High offer Career and Technical Education pathways including metals and fabrication, automotive technology, and International Baccalaureate.

Charter

Place-Based Education

Muddy Creek Charter School uses the local environment and community as a starting point for teaching across the curriculum, connecting learning to real-world context through outdoor education.
