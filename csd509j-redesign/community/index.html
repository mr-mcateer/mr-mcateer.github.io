Employment Opportunities

Join a team of 917 professionals making a difference every day

Corvallis School District 509J is one of the largest employers in Benton County, with 917 staff members serving nearly 6,000 students across 13 schools. We are always looking for passionate, dedicated individuals to join our team.

Open positions span a range of roles, including certified teachers, classified support staff, administrative and leadership positions, and substitute teachers and assistants. Whether you are an experienced educator or just beginning your career, there is a place for you here.

View Open Positions

Teaching Positions

Classified

Facility Use

Community organizations can request use of school buildings and grounds

Corvallis School District welcomes community use of school facilities when not in use for district programs. Gyms, auditoriums, fields, and meeting rooms are available for civic, recreational, and educational purposes.

Request Process

  1. Submit a request — Complete the facility use application form
  2. Review — The Facilities team reviews availability and requirements
  3. Approval — Receive confirmation with any applicable fees and guidelines

Facilities & Maintenance

For facility use requests, scheduling questions, or information about available spaces, contact the Facilities & Maintenance department.

Get Involved

There are many ways to contribute to our schools and students

Volunteer

Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteers play a vital role in Corvallis schools. Help in classrooms, libraries, lunchrooms, and at school events. Background checks are required for all volunteers working with students.

Contact your school office to get started

Organizations

Parent-Teacher Organizations

Each school has an active PTO or PTA that organizes fundraisers, family events, and classroom support. PTOs are a great way to connect with other families and directly support your school.

Check with your school for meeting times

Finance

Budget Committee

Community members can serve on the district Budget Committee, which reviews the proposed annual budget and provides recommendations to the Board. Vacancies are announced each spring.

Applications open annually

Advisory

Community Advisory Committees

The district convenes advisory committees on topics such as equity, safety, facilities planning, and curriculum. These committees bring together educators, families, and community members to inform policy.

Openings posted on district website
