Who Should I Contact?

Find the right department for your question or concern

I need to enroll my child

Contact your neighborhood school directly, or call the district office at (541) 757-5811 for enrollment assistance. Visit the Families page for enrollment forms and required documents.

I have a question about my child's bus route

Contact Student Transportation at (541) 757-5838. You can also check routes using the My Ride K-12 app, available on the Transportation page.

I need to report a building maintenance issue

Contact Facilities & Maintenance at (541) 757-5871. For urgent safety concerns, notify your building principal immediately.

I have a question about school meals

Contact Food & Nutrition at (541) 757-5841. Menus, free/reduced meal applications, and dietary accommodation information are available on the School Menus page.

I need IT or technology support (staff)

Contact Technology Services at (541) 757-5848. Staff can also submit a help desk ticket through the Staff Resources page.

I want to apply for a job

Contact Human Resources at (541) 757-5811 or visit the Employment page for current openings, application instructions, and information about working at CSD 509J.

I have a concern about my child's education

Start with your child's teacher. If the concern is not resolved, contact the building principal. For district-level support, reach Student Growth & Experience at (541) 757-5811. See the Feedback & Concerns section below for the full escalation process.

I want to use a school facility

Contact Facilities & Maintenance at (541) 757-5871 to inquire about facility rentals, community use permits, and scheduling availability.

Feedback & Concerns

How to share feedback or raise a concern with the district

Corvallis School District values open communication with families and community members. If you have a concern about your child's experience, the district encourages you to start at the level closest to the issue and work through the established process.

Recommended Escalation Path
  1. Teacher or staff member — Begin with the person most directly involved
  2. Building principal — If unresolved, contact the school principal
  3. District office — Contact the appropriate department at (541) 757-5811
  4. Superintendent — For concerns not resolved through previous steps
  5. School Board — Formal complaints may be addressed to the Board through established policy

Board policies KL (Public Complaints) and KLD (Public Complaints About District Personnel) outline the formal complaint process. Copies of these policies are available through the district office or on the Board page.
