Influencer Tier Definitions

Frisbeam's influencer strategy is built on high-engagement creators who generate genuine excitement around nighttime outdoor play. The LED glow aesthetic is inherently visual and shareable, which means even smaller creators can drive outsized content performance when the product lights up on camera.

Tier Followers Cost / Post Engagement Partnership Mix Strategic Value
Nano 1K -- 10K $50 -- $150 + free product 5 -- 8% 40% Highest engagement, authentic feel, genuine word-of-mouth within tight-knit communities
Micro 10K -- 50K $200 -- $500 + free product 3 -- 5% 35% Strong niche authority, trusted recommendations, best ROI per dollar
Mid-Tier 50K -- 250K $500 -- $2,000 2 -- 4% 20% Broader reach, awareness spikes, bridges niche to mainstream
Macro 250K+ $2,000 -- $5,000 1 -- 3% 5% Mass awareness, selective partnerships only, best for tent-pole moments
Allocation Strategy 75% of the influencer budget targets Nano and Micro tiers. These creators produce the most content per dollar, their audiences trust product recommendations, and the LED glow footage they capture at night consistently outperforms daytime product content in engagement metrics. Macro spend is reserved for proven viral opportunities only.

Target Creator Archetypes

Five personas represent the ideal Frisbeam creator partner. Each archetype aligns with a core use case for the LED disc game and maps to a platform where that content performs best.

🎉

The Party Host

Lifestyle & entertainment creators | TikTok & Instagram

These creators film group hangouts, house parties, backyard get-togethers, and social events. Their content thrives on energy, laughter, and group dynamics. Frisbeam's LED glow becomes a natural centerpiece in their party footage -- it looks incredible on camera without any extra effort.

  • Content angle: "Wait until you see this at night" -- unboxing at a party, crowd reactions to the glow
  • Why they work: Natural product placement in social scenes audiences already want to watch
  • Platforms: TikTok (primary), Instagram Reels
⛰

The Outdoor Explorer

Camping, hiking & adventure creators | YouTube & Instagram

Adventure creators who document camping trips, beach weekends, and outdoor excursions. Their audience follows them for gear recommendations and outdoor lifestyle inspiration. Frisbeam slots into campsite evening footage and beach sunset sessions as the activity that extends the adventure after dark.

  • Content angle: "Camp game that glows" -- playing under the stars, campfire to Frisbeam transition shots
  • Why they work: Shows the product in aspirational outdoor settings where the LED effect looks most dramatic
  • Platforms: YouTube (long-form), Instagram Reels
🎮

The Game Reviewer

Board game, yard game & toy review channels | YouTube

Dedicated reviewers who break down game mechanics, build quality, and value for money. Their audiences are active buyers who trust detailed, honest assessments. A positive review from this archetype acts as a powerful trust signal that converts browsers into buyers.

  • Content angle: "Full review and gameplay" -- unboxing, build quality close-ups, rules explanation, real gameplay footage
  • Why they work: Detailed product showcase drives high-intent traffic; their audience buys what they recommend
  • Platforms: YouTube (primary)
🎶

The Festival Goer

Music festival & nightlife content | TikTok & Instagram

Creators who live for music festivals, outdoor concerts, and nightlife experiences. Their content is visually rich, fast-paced, and built around atmosphere. The LED glow of Frisbeam is a perfect match for their aesthetic -- neon, night, and energy. Festival campgrounds and pre-show fields are ideal gameplay locations.

  • Content angle: "Festival campground vibes" -- glowing discs flying at dusk, crowd gathering to watch
  • Why they work: Perfect alignment with the glow-at-night aesthetic; content feels native, not sponsored
  • Platforms: TikTok (primary), Instagram Stories
🤝

The Couple / Friend Group

Relationship & friend content creators | TikTok & Instagram

Creators who document their lives with a partner or close friend group. Content focuses on activities they do together -- date nights, weekend plans, group challenges. Frisbeam becomes the shared experience in their content, showing the social connection the product creates.

  • Content angle: "Date night / friend group challenge" -- couples or friends competing, genuine reactions, rematch energy
  • Why they work: Shows the social experience of playing together; audience imagines themselves in the same scenario
  • Platforms: TikTok (primary), Instagram Reels

Outreach Templates

First contact should feel personal, specific, and low-pressure. The goal is to start a conversation, not close a deal. Always reference a specific piece of content the creator has posted before reaching out.

Initial DM Template (Casual, Short) Hey [First Name]! Loved your [specific post/video]. We just built an LED disc game called Frisbeam -- it glows at night and looks insane on camera. Would love to send you a free set to try with your crew. No strings. Interested?
✉

Just circling back on my message from last week. We're launching Frisbeam -- an LED-powered disc game designed for exactly the kind of [outdoor hangouts / nighttime parties / adventures] you create content around. The discs and targets glow in the dark, and the footage creators have been capturing at night has been outperforming their normal content.

Happy to send you a free Glow Set ($199 value) to try. If you love it and want to share it, we can set up an affiliate link so you earn commission on any sales. If it's not your thing, keep the set either way.

Would you be down?

Best,
[Your name]
Frisbeam Team

Compensation Models

Flexible compensation structures allow Frisbeam to partner with creators at every level. The goal is to make every partnership feel like genuine enthusiasm rather than a paid transaction.

Model Creator Receives Cost to Frisbeam Best For
Product-Only Free Glow Set ($199 value) + unique discount code to share ~$85 COGS + shipping Nano creators, initial seeding waves, creators who reach out organically
Flat Fee + Product Free set + negotiated cash rate per deliverable $200 -- $2,000 per post (tier-dependent) Micro and Mid-tier creators with proven engagement or conversion history
Revenue Share 10 -- 15% commission on all sales tracked through unique affiliate code Variable (performance-based) All tiers; especially creators with high purchase-intent audiences
Ambassador Monthly retainer + free product + exclusive early access to new products $500 -- $2,000/month + product Top-performing repeat partners with consistent content output and proven sales attribution
Preferred Entry Point Every new partnership starts with Product-Only or Revenue Share. Flat fee arrangements are only offered after a creator has demonstrated performance with an initial product-seeded collaboration. This ensures authentic content and protects the brand from paying for posts that feel hollow.

Campaign Tracking Framework

Every creator partnership must be measurable. The tracking infrastructure below ensures accurate attribution from first impression through final purchase, with clear reporting cadences to optimize spend over time.

Metrics Per Creator

Metric What It Measures How It Is Tracked
Unique Discount Code Usage Direct-attributed conversions and revenue per creator Format: CREATOR15 (creator name + discount %). Shopify Collabs or GoAffPro integration
UTM Link Clicks Traffic source, platform performance, click-through behavior ?utm_source=influencer&utm_medium=[platform]&utm_campaign=[creator_name] via GA4
Engagement Rate (Likes + comments + shares + saves) / total views Native analytics from TikTok Creator Marketplace, Meta Brand Collabs Manager
Follower Growth Frisbeam account growth correlated with creator post timing Track @frisbeam follower count before/after each creator post goes live
Content Saves / Shares Intent signals stronger than likes -- indicates future purchase consideration Platform-native insights; prioritize creators with high save-to-view ratios

Tools

  • Unique promo codes per creator -- direct attribution for every order
  • UTM parameters -- full-funnel tracking from click to checkout in Google Analytics (GA4)
  • Platform-native analytics -- TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube insights for reach, views, and engagement
  • Affiliate dashboard -- Shopify Collabs, GoAffPro, or Refersion for commission tracking and payout management

Reporting Cadence

  • Monthly: Performance review per active creator -- engagement rate, attributed orders, cost per acquisition, content quality score (internal 1-5 rating)
  • Quarterly: Full program audit -- ROI analysis by tier and archetype, budget rebalancing, strategy adjustment, top performer identification for ambassador upgrades

Legal & Compliance

All influencer partnerships must comply with FTC guidelines and protect both the brand and the creator. Clear terms prevent disputes and build long-term trust.

FTC Disclosure Requirements

Every piece of sponsored or gifted content must include a clear, conspicuous disclosure. The FTC requires disclosure to be unavoidable to the viewer -- not buried in hashtags or below the fold.

  • Gifted product (no payment): #gifted or #frisbeampartner in the first line of the caption
  • Paid partnership: #ad in the first line of the caption, or use platform-native paid partnership labels
  • Affiliate links: Disclose the affiliate relationship ("I earn a commission if you buy through my link")
  • Video content: Verbal disclosure within the first 30 seconds of the video, plus written disclosure in the caption
Term Standard Position
Content Usage Rights Frisbeam retains the right to repost, boost (Spark Ads / Partnership Ads), and use creator content on owned channels for 12 months from publication. Creator retains ownership of the original content.
Exclusivity No exclusivity required for product-only partnerships. Flat fee and ambassador agreements include a 30-day exclusivity window (no competing LED game or disc game brand posts within 30 days of Frisbeam content).
Payment Terms Net 30 from content publication date. Affiliate commissions paid monthly on the 1st for the prior month's tracked sales.
Revisions One round of feedback before publication. Frisbeam may request factual corrections (incorrect product name, missing disclosure) but will never request script changes that compromise the creator's authentic voice.
Termination Either party may terminate with 7 days written notice. Outstanding compensation for completed deliverables remains payable regardless of termination.
Non-Negotiable FTC disclosure is mandatory on every piece of content. Frisbeam will not publish, boost, or compensate any content that lacks proper disclosure. Creators who refuse to disclose are removed from the program immediately. Protecting both the brand and the creator from regulatory risk is not optional.
