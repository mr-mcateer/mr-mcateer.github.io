6. Key Assumptions & Risks

The projections above depend on several assumptions that carry downside risk. Each is monitored and has a defined mitigation path.

Seasonal Demand Concentration Outdoor games skew heavily toward spring and summer. Q4/Q1 revenue may drop 40-60% from peak. Mitigation: build holiday gifting campaigns and indoor-play content for shoulder seasons. LED glow effect gives Frisbeam a natural advantage in dark winter evenings.
Rising Ad Costs Meta and TikTok CPMs trend upward 15-20% year-over-year. If paid CAC rises above $55, the conservative scenario ratio drops below 5:1. Mitigation: invest early in organic flywheel (SEO, email, referral program) to reduce paid channel dependency over time.
Competitor Entry The LED outdoor game segment is nascent. Success will attract fast-followers and knock-offs within 12-18 months. Mitigation: build brand equity and community before competitors can replicate. Patent-pending LED disc design provides a defensibility window.
Supply Chain for LED Components LED modules and rechargeable battery packs are sourced from a single supplier. Lead times are currently 8-10 weeks. Mitigation: qualify a secondary supplier by month 3 and maintain 6-week safety stock to buffer against disruptions.
