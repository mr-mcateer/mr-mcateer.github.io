Seasonal Calendar

LED outdoor game demand follows strong seasonal patterns. The calendar front-loads spend into peak summer months while maintaining year-round brand presence and capturing the holiday gifting window.

Quarter Months Spend Share Annual $ Strategic Focus
Q1 Jan - Mar 15% $27,000 Brand awareness, email list building, pre-season hype. Creative production for spring ramp. Retarget warm audiences from holiday.
Q2 Apr - Jun 30% $54,000 Launch campaigns, "summer nights" creative, influencer seeding begins. Memorial Day and graduation gifting pushes.
Q3 Jul - Sep 35% $63,000 Peak season. Maximum prospecting spend, retargeting, UGC amplification, festival presence. July 4th, Labor Day, tailgate season kickoff.
Q4 Oct - Dec 20% $36,000 Holiday gifting. Black Friday/Cyber Monday promotions, gift guide placements, "perfect outdoor gift" messaging, tailgate season.
Peak Spend Window 65% of the annual budget deploys between April and September. Creative assets, influencer contracts, and event logistics must be locked by March 15 to avoid bottlenecks during ramp.

Content Pillars

All creative across every channel maps to three content pillars. This framework ensures consistent messaging while giving each platform enough flexibility to feel native.

60%

Glow Moments

60% of all content

The flagship pillar. Gameplay footage shot at night, LED disc trails against dark skies, group fun under string lights, slow-motion catches with the glow trail visible. This is the content that stops the scroll and drives shares.

  • Night gameplay sessions with friends and family
  • Slow-motion glow trail captures
  • Group reaction shots and celebration moments
  • Backyard, beach, park, and tailgate settings
20%

Behind the Beam

20% of all content

The authority and trust builder. Content that showcases the LED technology, product design process, material choices, and the founder story. Positions Frisbeam as a premium product worth the investment.

  • Product design and engineering deep-dives
  • LED technology explainers and durability demos
  • Founder story and brand mission
  • Manufacturing and quality control process
20%

Community

20% of all content

The social proof engine. User-submitted gameplay, tournament highlights, tips and strategy, and reposts from the community. This pillar scales organically as the player base grows.

  • User-submitted gameplay and photos
  • Tournament highlights and leaderboard recaps
  • Tips, tricks, and throwing technique guides
  • Community challenges and hashtag campaigns
Pillar Balance Rule Every weekly content calendar must reflect the 60/20/20 split within a +/- 10% tolerance. If Glow Moments content drops below 50% of weekly output, pause other pillars until the ratio resets. The glow footage is the brand's competitive moat.
