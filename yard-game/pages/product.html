Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Here are the ones we hear most. If yours is not here, reach out.

How long does the battery last?

The Glow and Pro sets get 8-12 hours per charge, depending on which LED mode you are running. Solid color modes last the longest. Pulse and multi-cycle modes use a bit more power but still easily outlast any session. Full recharge takes under 2 hours via the included USB-C dock.

Can I play in the rain?

Yes. Every LED component is rated IP65, which means it handles rain, splashes, and heavy dew without any issues. We would not recommend submerging them in a pool, but everything short of that is fair game. Dry them off when you are done and they will last for years.

How long does shipping take?

Most orders ship within 1-2 business days from our warehouse. Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days, and shipping is free on every order over $99. Need it faster? Expedited 2-day shipping is available at checkout for $14.99.

What is the difference between the three sets?

The Starter ($129) gives you 2 single-color LED discs, 2 targets, and a drawstring bag. It is a great way to try the game. The Glow ($199) upgrades you to 4 multi-color discs, lit targets, a charging dock, and a padded case. The Pro ($299) goes all in with 6 discs, reactive targets, fast-charge dock with spare batteries, a hard-shell case, and a Bluetooth speaker mount. Most people land on Glow.

What does the warranty cover?

Every Frisbeam set comes with a 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects in the discs, targets, charging equipment, and case. If an LED dies, a target cracks, or anything fails under normal use, we replace it at no cost. Normal wear from gameplay is not covered, but honestly that is just proof you are using it right.

How do I change the LED color modes?

Each disc and target has a small button that cycles through the available color modes. Quick press switches colors. Long press toggles pulse mode on or off. The Pro set targets also have a reactive mode that flashes on impact. No app needed. No pairing. Just press and play.
