Key Research Findings

Highlights from across the nine deliverables.

Market

White Space Opportunity

No single competitor combines integrated LED lighting, structured disc game mechanics, premium build quality, and true portability. The $85M illuminated yard games market is growing at 12% CAGR with no category leader.

Economics

Strong Unit Economics

71% blended gross margin at $199 AOV. Blended CAC of $38 yields an 8.2:1 LTV-to-CAC ratio against a 3:1 healthy DTC benchmark. Sub-month payback period with $141 net margin per order.

Audience

Two High-Value Cohorts

Night Owl (28%) and Experience Collector (22%) segments represent 50% of the market but 53% of total spend. LED glow and shareable aesthetics are the top messaging drivers across both groups.
