Toolbox Certification Day

Mr. McAteer -- Automotive Manufacturing -- Crescent Valley HS
Inventory, organize, and certify your toolbox. Every tool must be present, functional, and in the right place. This is graded.
  1. Empty the toolbox completely. Lay every item on your bench. Sort by type.
  2. Audit each tool against the Master Tool List (laminated, inside the lid). Mark present, rate condition, assign action code.
  3. Pull extras. Anything NOT on the list goes to the Orphan Bin (front bench). Log it below. To ID unknowns: (1) check page 2, (2) scan the QR code, (3) ask a senior.
  4. Clean and organize. Wipe every tool. Top drawer = sockets/ratchet/extensions. Middle = wrenches. Bottom = screwdrivers, pliers, specialty.
  5. Get certified. Bring this form to Mr. McAteer. He spot-checks 3 items. Match = pass.
Seniors: you are auditing the teacher tool cabinet using this same process. Float to help student boxes after they have checked the reference materials.
