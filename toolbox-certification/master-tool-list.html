Master Tool List

Predator 212 Engine -- Small Engine Manufacturing
20 required tools per toolbox. Laminate this page and tape inside the lid.
Top Drawer Sockets, ratchet, extensions
Middle Drawer Combination wrenches
Bottom Drawer Screwdrivers, pliers, specialty
# Tool Qty Purpose (Why It's Here)
⚙ Sockets & Drive -- Top Drawer
1 Ratchet, 3/8" drive 1 Primary driver for all socket work. The backbone of the kit.
2 Extension, 3" (3/8" drive) 1 Reaches bolts in tight spots near the block.
3 Extension, 6" (3/8" drive) 1 Reaches deeper fasteners, head bolts, and under-shroud hardware.
4 8mm socket 1 Valve cover, air filter housing, small shroud bolts.
5 10mm socket 1 Most-used size. Shroud, blower housing, fuel tank mount. Guard it.
The 10mm socket is the most commonly lost tool in any shop. Know where yours is at all times.
6 12mm socket 1 Head bolts, side cover, muffler mount.
7 13mm socket 1 Flywheel nut (with holder). Some aftermarket bolts.
8 14mm socket 1 Spark plug (if no dedicated spark plug socket), larger mounts.
🔧 Wrenches -- Middle Drawer
9 8mm combination wrench 1 Backup for 8mm socket. Holds bolt head while socket drives the nut.
10 10mm combination wrench 1 Backup for 10mm socket. Hold-and-turn situations.
11 12mm combination wrench 1 Head bolt backup. Used when ratchet clearance is tight.
12 13mm combination wrench 1 Flywheel work, larger fastener backup.
🛠 Screwdrivers -- Bottom Drawer
13 Phillips #2 screwdriver 1 Carburetor screws, governor arm, throttle linkage adjustments.
14 Flathead screwdriver, 3/16" 1 Idle mixture screw, prying gaskets, general scraping.
✂ Pliers -- Bottom Drawer
15 Needle-nose pliers 1 Governor spring, fuel line clips, small cotter pins, wire routing.
16 Slip-joint pliers 1 General gripping, pulling stuck fuel lines, holding parts.
★ Specialty -- Bottom Drawer
17 Adjustable wrench, 8" 1 Odd-size fasteners, holding flats while torquing. Versatile backup.
18 Feeler gauge set 1 Valve lash adjustment (0.005"-0.007" intake, 0.005"-0.007" exhaust).
19 Magnetic parts tray 1 Keeps bolts, nuts, and small parts from rolling away during teardown.
20 Wire brush 1 Cleans gasket surfaces, rust, carbon buildup from mating faces.
Total Required Tools: 20 All metric (mm) except feeler gauges (SAE thousandths)
Precision tools (torque wrench, compression tester, micrometer, calipers) are shared checkout items -- not stored in individual toolboxes. See Mr. McAteer to check these out when needed for measurement and final assembly.
