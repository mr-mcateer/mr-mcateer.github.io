The three intervals that save your engine. Oil & filter 3K-5K (conventional) or 7.5K-10K (synthetic). Tire rotation 5K-7.5K + monthly pressure check. Brake fluid flush every 2-3 years -- "nobody does this, until the pedal goes to the floor."
