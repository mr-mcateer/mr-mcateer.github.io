Data & Privacy

GDPR Privacy Statement

The FCoV-23 International Research Consortium collects only the information you provide through the contact form, which is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, share, or use your data for any purpose beyond direct communication related to our research.

In accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold. Contact us at info@fcov23.org for any data rights requests.

This website does not use tracking cookies, analytics services, or advertising networks.
